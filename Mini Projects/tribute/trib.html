Billy Butcher

William J. Butcher, or "Billy the Butcher", is an antihero and later antagonist in the Amazon Prime series The Boys,

About the Legend

  • Leader of The Boys: Billy Butcher is the ruthless and determined leader of the vigilante group known as "The Boys," dedicated to taking down corrupt superheroes.
  • Personal Vendetta: His mission is driven by a personal vendetta against Homelander, who he believes is responsible for his wife Becca's disappearance and presumed death.
  • Military Background: Butcher's background in the British Special Air Service (SAS) contributes to his tactical expertise and combat skills.
  • Complex Morality: He is committed to his mission, but his methods are often morally ambiguous, involving extreme violence and manipulation.
  • Charismatic and Cunning: Known for his charismatic and cunning nature, Butcher can manipulate and persuade others to join his cause.
  • Wife's Disappearance: The disappearance of his wife, Becca Butcher, is a pivotal event in his life, driving his deep-seated hatred for superheroes.
  • Homelander Rivalry: Butcher's primary antagonist is Homelander, the leader of The Seven, with their intense and personal rivalry fueling much of the show's conflict.
  • Resourcefulness: He is incredibly resourceful, using any means necessary to gather information, secure weapons, and execute his plans against the Supes.
  • Traumatic Past: Butcher's childhood was marked by trauma, including an abusive father and a violent environment, shaping his aggressive and mistrustful nature.
  • Protective Nature: Despite his ruthless methods, Butcher shows a protective side towards those he considers allies, especially towards Hughie and his deceased wife, Becca.
